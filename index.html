Our Journey

How We Met

September 14, 2022

A Blessed Beginning

Our paths crossed at a charity event in London. Zayn was setting up tables, and Layla was arranging the decorations. A simple request for assistance led to a conversation about our dreams, families, and values. It was a meeting designed by destiny, filled with mutual respect from the very first moment.

October 12, 2024

The Proposal & Proposal Acceptance

With the blessings of our families, Zayn formally proposed to Layla during a quiet evening walk in a beautifully lit conservatory. Surrounded by warm lanterns and glowing flowers, we promised to support and guide each other in faith and love for the rest of our days.
